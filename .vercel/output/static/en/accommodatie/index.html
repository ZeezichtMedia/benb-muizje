Accommodation

Details about our holiday home, facilities, and amenities.

Pure enjoyment

Oasis of peace near the coast

Everything you need for a carefree holiday on the Zeeland coast. The house is attractively furnished and fully equipped.

Discover

Living & Cooking

The sunny living room is furnished with a three- and two-seater sofa. Through the sliding doors, you have direct access to the sunny terrace with furniture and an awning. The open kitchen is fully equipped, including an induction hob, oven/microwave, dishwasher, and fridge with freezer compartment.

Discover

Sleeping & Bathing

Our comfortable holiday home features two bedrooms with four single box-spring mattresses. In the hallway, you will find a separate toilet and a bathroom with a shower, washbasin, and storage cabinet.

Discover

Comfort & Convenience

The holiday home is heated by central heating, ensuring you stay warm even in the colder months. Everything is on one level, creating an accessible and spacious feeling.
